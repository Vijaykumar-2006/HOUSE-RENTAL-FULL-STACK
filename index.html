Welcome to the Homely Management System

Homely is a state-of-the-art house rental dashboard designed to streamline the leasing experience for landlords, property managers, and tenants. By automating rent collection, tenant screening, maintenance requests, and contract creation, Homely ensures that property management remains modern, organized, and secure.

Our Vision

To redefine residential renting by creating a seamless, transparent, and digitally empowered ecosystem where finding and managing homes is stress-free.

Our Mission

To provide managers and tenants with robust automated tools, real-time analytics, and instant support to ensure safe and happy housing communities globally.

Platform Key Features

  • Comprehensive Tenant Background Screening: Performs automated identity checks, credit scores, and reference verification.
  • Automated Multi-Channel Rent Collection: Allows tenants to pay via credit card, ACH, or mobile wallet with automatic landlord payout.
  • Instant Digital Lease Agreements: Generate customized, legally binding lease drafts in minutes and secure electronic signatures.
  • Real-Time Maintenance Request Tracker: Submit maintenance requests, upload photographs, and monitor contractors live.
  • Smart Rental Inventory Mapping: Create 3D or visual inventories of houses during move-in and check-out to calculate deposit deductions.

Offered Services

  1. 24/7 Concierge Support Services: In-app emergency line for electrical failures, gas leaks, water damage, or locks replacement.
  2. Complete Legal and Compliance Guidance: Expert lawyers inspect and review rental disputes, eviction procedures, and tax audits.
  3. Property Listing Optimization: Professional photography and automated syndication to major real estate portals.
  4. Financial Bookkeeping & Statements: Automatic ledger records, quarterly tax documentation, and detailed cashflow analysis.
  5. Refurbishment and interior styling consultation: Help homeowners remodel apartments to maximize monthly rental valuations.
